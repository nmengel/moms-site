Our Philosophy

Your children’s well-being is our main priority. Our board certified doctors specialize in the care of children and adolescents. We are in a group practice to ensure that help is available at all times to meet your children’s needs.

In our practice, each patient is assigned to a primary physician of their choice. We make every effort for your child to see their primary physician for their well-child visits. However, night and weekend coverage is handled on a rotating basis by our four physicians. This includes newborn care at the hospital and inpatient care on the pediatric floor of Tallahassee Memorial Hospital.

We strongly believe in the continuity of care for our patients. Our aim is to provide truly comprehensive health care through young adulthood.

Doctors
