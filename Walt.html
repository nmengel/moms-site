Walter Bunnell III, MD grew up in St. Augustine, Florida. He attended University of Florida medical school in Gainesville, Florida, and completed his Pediatric residency at University of Florida Health Shands Hospital. After residency, he served as Chief Resident and Assistant Professor at Shands before moving to Tallahassee and joining Timberlane Pediatrics, P.A., where he practiced general pediatrics from 1982 until 1999. In 1999, he formed Professional Park Pediatrics, P.A. He is passionate about following his patients from newborn care to young adulthood. He particularly likes seeing patients grow up, have children, and return to him with second and third-generation patients. Outside work he enjoys gardening, baking bread, traveling and attending Gator football games.
