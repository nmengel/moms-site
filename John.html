John Elzie MD grew up in North Florida and graduated from Leon High School in Tallahassee. He attended medical school at the University of South Florida and completed his pediatric residency in Gainesville at the University of Florida. He moved back to Tallahassee in 2001 with his wife and three children to join Professional Park Pediatrics. He enjoys practicing pediatrics with special emphasis on preventive care and continuity of care. Outside work he enjoys hiking, kayaking, and scuba diving in Florida and outside the US when he gets a chance.
