Your children’s well-being is our main priority. Our board certified doctors specialize in the care of children and adolescents. We are prepared to take care of all your children's health needs: well-child check-ups, illnesses and injuries. We are in a group practice to ensure that help is available at all times to meet your children’s needs.

Besides having an on-call physician, our physicians continue to see newborns at the hospital. In our practice, each patient is assigned to a primary physician of their choice. We make every effort for your child to see their primary physician for their well-child visits. However, night and weekend coverage are handled on a rotating basis by our four physicians, and you will be cared for by the on-call physicians. This includes newborn care at the hospital as well as in-patient hospitalization on the pediatric floor of Tallahassee Memorial Hospital.

We strongly believe in the continuity of care for our patients. Our aim is to provide truly comprehensive health care though young adulthood.
