Our Philosophy

Your children’s well-being is our main priority. Our board certified doctors specialize in the care of children and adolescents. We are in a group practice to ensure that help is available at all times to meet your children’s needs.

In our practice, each patient is assigned to a primary physician of their choice. We make every effort for your child to see their primary physician for their well-child visits. However, night and weekend coverage is handled on a rotating basis by our four physicians. This includes newborn care at the hospital and inpatient care on the pediatric floor of Tallahassee Memorial Hospital.

We strongly believe in the continuity of care for our patients. Our aim is to provide truly comprehensive health care though young adulthood.

Doctors

Nectar Aintablian MD grew up in Lebanon and attended American University of Beirut for Medical school. After her residency in Pediatrics at Buffalo Children’s Hospital she served as chief resident there. She then completed a fellowship in Pediatric Infectious Diseases at UC San Diego. Walter Bunnell III, MD grew up in St. Augustine, Florida. He attended University of Florida medical school in Gainesville, Florida, and completed his Pediatric residency at University of Florida Health Shands Hospital. After residency, he served as Chief Resident and Assistant Professor at Shands before moving to Tallahassee and joining Timberlane Pediatrics, P.A., where he practiced general pediatrics from 1982 until 1999. In 1999, he formed Professional Park Pediatrics, P.A. He is passionate about following his patients from newborn care to young adulthood. He particularly likes seeing patients grow up, have children, and return to him with second and third-generation patients. Outside work he enjoys gardening, baking bread, traveling and attending Gator football games.
