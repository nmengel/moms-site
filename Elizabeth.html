Elizabeth Hightower was raised in Tallahassee and is a Leon High School alumna. She earned a Bachelor of Social Work and a Master of Social Work from FSU before moving to Atlanta to work in the fields of mental health and pharmaceutical sales for 10 years. She returned to Tallahassee in 2007 and earned a Bachelor of Science in Nursing from Tallahassee Community College and a Master of Science in Nursing (specializing in pediatric primary care) from the University of South Alabama. She has worked at Professional Park Pediatrics since 2016 and loves working with children of all ages. She and her husband have three teenage daughters. Her family's hobbies include traveling, music, and watching or playing soccer. Her daughters and husband love to play golf so she hopes to add that to her list of activities soon.
