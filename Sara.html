Sara Barber is a native of Tallahassee who graduated from Leon High School and then went on to earn a Bachelor of Science in Nursing and a Master of Science in Nursing from Florida State University. Her experience includes working as a Registered Nurse in Labor and Delivery at Tallahassee Memorial Hospital. Sara has been with Professional Park Pediatrics since September of 2002, working a part-time schedule while raising her three kids. Sara loves seeing newborns and answering the questions of new parents, having in-depth chats with 4-year-olds about their favorite superheroes and counseling tweens and teens through the challenges of growing up. In her spare time Sara enjoys spending time with her family--one husband, two dogs, and three kids--as well as traveling, reading and watching her kids play a lot of soccer.
