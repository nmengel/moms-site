Newborns


Fever

Any infant under three months of age who has a fever greater than or equal to 100.4 degrees rectally may be in danger of having a serious illness. We need to be informed of such an infant immediately so we can make arrangements to evaluate them.

Weight Checks

Weight checks for newborns are done on a walk-in basis, and do not require appointments. Nurses are most available for weight check walk-ins from 9-10:30AM or 2-3:30PM.
